Strategy Workspace

Page · Strategy · 5 min

The central hub for managing all your quantitative strategies — create, browse, rename, delete, and dive into the detail page for deep editing.

TL;DR

The Strategy Workspace is where you start in AlphaLoop. It shows a grid of strategy cards, each displaying the name, status (draft / active / paused / archived), type, and source. Click a card to enter the detail page; the "New Strategy" button in the top right opens a creation panel. After creation, you are taken directly to the detail page where you can write DSL rules, open the canvas, run backtests, or start paper trading.

Who uses it & when

Every user begins here. The first time, the list is empty with an onboarding message and a create button. Each time you return to AlphaLoop, this workspace is your home base — pick a strategy to edit, or clean up old ones. The status badges (Total / Draft / Active) at the top give you a quick snapshot of your portfolio.

Page Anatomy

  1. ① Strategy card grid — Each card shows the name, status indicator (green=active, yellow=draft, gray=archived), type badge, and a short description. Right-click to open the context menu for rename or delete. Click the card to enter the detail page.
  2. ② Status badges — The header shows Total, Draft, and Active counts. A quick glance at your portfolio health.
  3. ③ Create panel (overlay) — Slides in from the top when you click "New Strategy". Enter a name and click create. The panel uses a frosted glass background and closes when you tap outside.
  4. ④ Detail page — After creation or clicking a card, you land on the strategy detail page with 10 tabs: Overview, DSL Rules, Canvas, Backtest, Versions, Runs, Signals, Paper Trading, Risk, and Growth.

Reading the Key Metrics

  • Status indicator — Green = active (connected to the exchange and executing), yellow = draft (still being edited), orange = paused, gray = archived (retired).
  • Strategy type — The algorithmic category (trend following, mean reversion, grid, arbitrage, etc.) helps you sort and filter.
  • Source — How the strategy was created: Custom (hand-written), Template (from the template library), or AI Generated.

Common Actions, Step by Step

  1. Create a strategy — Click "New Strategy" in the top right, type a name (e.g. "Trend Follow v4"), and click "Create Strategy". You are taken to the detail page automatically.
  2. Enter the detail page — Click any strategy card in the grid. The detail page shows the name, status, type, source at the top, and 10 functional tabs below.
  3. Rename a strategy — Right-click a card, choose "Rename", enter the new name in the sheet, and save.
  4. Delete a strategy — Right-click a card, choose "Delete", and confirm in the dialog. This action cannot be undone.

Behind the Scenes

The strategy list is fetched via APIStrategiesV2.list() from the backend, returning an array of StrategyV2 objects. Creation calls api.create(name:) and updates AppState routing to navigate to the detail page on success. Status counts (draft/active) are computed client-side by filtering the viewModel.strategies array — no extra requests needed. The list refreshes via .task { await viewModel.load() } when the view appears.
