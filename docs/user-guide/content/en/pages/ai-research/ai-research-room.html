AI Research Room

Page · AI Research · 5 min

Enter an asset, and 7 AI researchers analyze it in parallel from different perspectives, producing a directional rating and trade recommendation.

TL;DR

The AI Research Room is the front door to AlphaLoop's "Researcher" role. Pick a trading pair and a research depth, and the AI runs 7 parallel analyses: bull case, bear case, technical, sentiment, news, fundamentals/on-chain, and risk assessment. It synthesizes everything into a directional call (bullish/bearish/neutral) with a confidence score. Results can be published as a signal or turned into a strategy draft with one click.

Who uses it & when

Every trader should visit the AI Research Room before entering a position, whether trading manually or running automated strategies. Use it when you want a quick read on an unfamiliar asset, or when you want an independent AI opinion on something you already follow. Research results can also feed into the signal fusion system as a signal source.

Page Anatomy

  1. ① Asset Input Area — Enter a trading pair (e.g., BTC/USDT) and select research depth via tabs: Quick (~30s scan), Standard (~2min analysis), Deep (~5min deep research). Quick-access buttons for popular symbols.
  2. ② 7 Parallel Perspectives — Seven independent research agents analyze simultaneously: Bull Case, Bear Case, Technical, Sentiment, News, Fundamentals/On-Chain, Risk. Each panel shows a confidence bar (green >= 70%, yellow 40-70%, red < 40%) and a summary.
  3. ③ Final Rating — Synthesized directional call (Bullish/Bearish/Neutral), overall confidence score, risk level, and trade recommendation. Two action buttons below: "Publish as Signal" (pushes to Signal Center) and "Generate Strategy Draft" (opens Strategy Workspace).

Reading the Key Metrics

  • Confidence Bar — Each perspective's confidence reflects how reliable that dimension is. Technical and on-chain analyses typically score higher (backed by quantitative data); sentiment analysis scores lower (social media noise). A low-confidence perspective isn't wrong — it just means there's insufficient data for a strong call.
  • Final Rating Confidence — Overall confidence across all 7 perspectives. Above 70% is a strong signal; 40-70% warrants caution; below 40% suggests waiting for more confirmation.
  • Risk Level — Low / Medium / High / Critical. Influences the suggested position size. A high-risk signal, even if directionally bullish, should be sized accordingly.

Common Actions, Step by Step

  1. Enter a trading pair in the input field (or tap a quick-access symbol).
  2. Choose research depth: Standard for first time, Quick for a checkup, Deep for thorough analysis.
  3. Tap "Start Research." A progress indicator shows the estimated wait time.
  4. When complete, read through the 7 perspective panels. Prioritize high-confidence perspectives.
  5. Check the final rating for direction and risk level.
  6. Tap "Publish as Signal" to push the result into the Signal Center for fusion scoring, or "Generate Strategy Draft" to continue editing in the Strategy Workspace.

Behind the Scenes

Tapping "Start Research" calls /api/ai-research/runs to create a research task. The backend uses tradingagents_adapter.py to invoke the TradingAgents multi-agent framework — each perspective runs as an independent research agent in parallel. If a PDF report was uploaded, rag_service.py first extracts strategy concepts and key indicators from the document to inject as research context. Results are fetched via polling. Generated strategies pass through code_safety.py for static security analysis (blocking dangerous imports like os, subprocess, and dangerous calls like eval, exec) before becoming deployable code.
